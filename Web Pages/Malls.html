CITY CENTER ALMAZA City Centre Almaza will become one of the key favoured destinations for shopping and entertainment in Helipolis. Almaza, meaning diamond in Arabic, is derived from the rich history of philosophies, planning and design aspirations from Heliopolis, which has been integrated into the planning and design of City Centre Almaza's shopping experience. Offering a wide variety of meticulously selected brands from a wide variety of retail mix, dining and leisure amenities, the mall will create a unique shopping enviornment for its visitors. Located amongst one of the strongest development locations in Cairo, on the north eastern quadrant of the junction between the Cairo-Suez Road and Nasr Road in Heliopolis, the mall offers easy accessibility in an affluent primary catchment area. City Centre Almaza will re-define the shopping landscape in Egypt to become the go-to shopping mall for residents in Helipolis. CITY CENTER ALMAZA
